About Eliksir Foundation

Eliksir Foundation is a not-for-profit serving the Greater Toronto Area with faith-rooted social services. We work in partnership with local organizations and volunteers to deliver practical supports and create opportunities for community wellbeing.

Programs

  • Food & Basic Needs: Community food distribution and grocery supports.
  • Housing Navigation: Support for people experiencing housing instability.
  • Mental Health & Counselling: Faith-sensitive referrals and group supports.
  • Youth & Family Programs: After-school, mentoring and family resources.

How we work

Our approach is client-centred, collaborative and respectful of diverse beliefs. We combine volunteer-driven delivery with partnerships across the GTA to maximize impact.
